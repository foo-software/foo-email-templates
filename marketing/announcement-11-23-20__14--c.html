Hello, we've made changes to our service that impact your account. We've decided to invest undivided focus on services having the largest unique value for our users - Lighthouse monitoring. For this reason, lack of adoption and more, we've decided to deprecate our Web Vitals and Feature Toggle service.

You can read about motivation and perspective moving forward here.

What this Means for You

  • Web Vitals will be unavailable in the future, but your data will still be accessible for three months in the Web Vitals dashboard.
  • If you choose to continue after the free trial ends, the cost of your monthly subscription will be $30 per month instead of $80.
  • We've added Web Vitals data as part of the Lighthouse results in the dashboard (via CrUX).

Thank You ❤️

We appreciate your enthusiasm in making the modern web a better place and hope you've found our services to be helpful. We're working on a redesign and developing a list of new features for 2021. As always, feel free to reach out with any questions, comments, or feature requests.
