Recent Incident and Status Pages

Hello! We've invested in a robust infrastructure (on Kubernetes) for highly available services with little to zero downtime. However, events from Thursday - Friday led to brief periods of downtime and degradation in Lighthouse performance audits. This issue was addressed, but put a spotlight on our need for better communication. In response, we've created the following spaces (also linked in the website navigation).

  • General discussion: Anything goes here. Not sure where to discuss a topic? Questions about how our website works? This is the place.
  • Incidents: In the event of user-impacting issues, we'll post real-time updates here.
    • Open incidents
    • Closed incidents
  • Bugs: Did you discover a bug? Open an issue here or review existing ones. We have a couple in progress, so have a look if you've noticed anything unusual.
  • Features: Have an idea that would really help your workflow? It doesn't hurt to put in a request. We're open to any ideas, even half-baked.

User Impact of this Recent Incident

Performance reports from 8/27 - 8/28 could show lower scores than expected. A few periods of downtime on Friday occurred, but for no longer than a few minutes at a time. The public API was functioning incorrectly for approximately 15 minutes in the middle of the day.

Conclusion

This was the first outage since our migration to Kubernetes about 6 months ago. These incidents will be rare, but hopefully we now offer a satisfactory space to promote collaboration and ensure we're transparent regarding bugs, features, incidents and general discussion. For complete details about this incident, check out the issue here.
